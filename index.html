CONCORDE OCEANLINKS is a comprehensive cargo management platform designed to optimize the cargo transfer process from origin to destination. This system automates the generation of quotations based on selected routes, streamlining the process of vendor communication by sending quotations directly via email. It also facilitates the efficient assignment of booked cargo to vendors, ensuring timely and accurate deliveries. The platform features a CBM (Cubic Meter) calculator that helps determine the most suitable containers for cargo, improving logistics efficiency. Additionally, it recommends vendors with competitive pricing, automates email quotations, and simplifies shipment assignments, reducing operational overhead and enhancing overall cost-effectiveness. By automating key processes, CONCORDE OCEANLINKS helps companies achieve faster, more reliable cargo management, ensuring an optimized supply chain.
